Welcome to Auxluraiña!

A safe place from the troubles of the modern world

Here, we believe in spreading information, protecting the environment, fostering creativity, creating communities, setting standards for safety, lifting up leaders, protecting the diverse, upholding an individual's right to choose, and most importantly, promoting everyday kindness.

Want to visit? Come whenever you wish. Our borders are completely open (aside from what the country on the other side does), you can come and stay as long as you like. If you apply for a citizenship, you will be assigned to a group, each with a appointed leader that you can talk to if you have any problems at all. Even if you do not wish to stay, we make information readily available, and even though no citizenship means you can not vote, you can still do basically everything else. Just be sure to convert your money to CHF, and remember to not bring lethal weapons, as they are strictly banned.
